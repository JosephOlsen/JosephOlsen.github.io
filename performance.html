Joseph Olsen
PORTFOLIO RESUME ABOUT CONTACT

PERFORMANCES

Nuthouse Improv Comedy
2013-2017

Over 30 performances showcasing a wide array of various short-form improv games. Voted Best Cast Member in 2015, I progressed from a cast member to regular host of shows and even began regularly leading weekly rehearsals. Audiences ranged in size from 20 to 800. As a tenured cast member, I advocated for increased diversity and inclusion at the casting level.



Olympia Improv Collective
2018-2019

Long-form improv performances based on audience interviews. I conducted most audience interviews before hosting several workshops for the troupe to train other members in how to guide conversational-style interviews. I also managed group finances as the creation and distribution of advertising materials for performances.



Pianist - Moby Dick Rehearsed
2013

Accompanist for stage performance of the play Moby Dick Rehearsed. Pieces ranged in style from pop song covers such as David Bowie’s Life on Mars to more classical pieces including Rachmaninoff’s Prelude in C# Minor Op. 3 No. 2.



Etsi Bravo – Weekly Standup Comedy
2016-2017

Performed weekly stand-up bits ranging in length from 4 to 12 minutes. While using general notes for my set, I never re-used the same exact joke as bits were improvised on the spot. This also made it easier to gage audience interaction and allowed for pivoting when necessary.
